Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 20, 2026

MailFlow ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our email automation service.

1. Information We Collect

When you authenticate with MailFlow using your Google account, we collect the following:

  • Your name and email address (from your Google profile)
  • OAuth access and refresh tokens to interact with Gmail on your behalf
  • Email content that is sent or received through the MailFlow interface

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information solely to provide the MailFlow service:

  • To authenticate you and maintain your session
  • To send and receive emails on your behalf via Gmail API
  • To display conversation history within the MailFlow interface
  • To enable AI-assisted email responses when the AI mode is activated

3. Data Storage

Your Gmail OAuth tokens and basic profile information are stored securely in our database to maintain your session across logins. Email content is processed in real time and is not permanently stored on our servers beyond what is necessary for the conversation interface.

4. Third-Party Services

MailFlow integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Google Gmail API — used to send, receive, and read emails on your behalf
  • Groq API — used to generate AI email responses when AI mode is enabled. Only the content of the relevant email thread is sent to Groq for processing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any other third parties.

5. Data Retention

Your account data is retained for as long as you use MailFlow. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at the email below.

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, including encrypted storage of OAuth tokens and session-based access controls. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Revoke Gmail access at any time via your Google Account settings

8. Children's Privacy

MailFlow is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
